Rules

This game is played in levels, with each new level being a different subpage of this website.
For example, if you check the URL, you'll see that this page is a subpage under /game of the main website thezoingler.github.io.
The /game part can be ignored, as it is only for organization on my end, but the introduction.html part is critical to how this game works.

Every time you entera new level (subpage), there will be a puzzle for you to solve. Once you solve it, you will be given a single word or phrase, that I will refer to as the keyword.
This keyword can be entered into the URL, in the place of introduction.html. This will take you to the next level.


As for the puzzles...
They will start fairly easy, in fact, these next couple of levels are designed as a tutorial of sorts to introduce the main concepts that will be used all throughout the game.
However, as you go on they will (hopefully) get harder.
Allow me to speak louder for the people in the back.

This game will get VERY DIFFICULT in later levels, so be prepared to spend time on this! BE PATIENT

Finally, above all other rules,
NO CHEATING
bad things happen to cheaters.
